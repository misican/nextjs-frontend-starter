## Ceremony Schedule Template (Per Sprint)
| Ceremony | Timing | Participants | Objective | Required Output |
|---|---|---|---|---|
| Sprint Planning | Day 1 - 10:00 | PO, TL, QA, FE Team, UX | Confirm scope, estimate, capacity | Approved sprint backlog + story commitment |
| Daily Standup | Weekdays - 09:30 | TL, QA, FE Team | Surface blockers and track flow | Updated board + impediment log |
| Backlog Refinement | Mid-sprint - 14:00 | PO, TL, QA, UX | Prepare next sprint candidate stories | Ready stories with G/W/T AC |
| Sprint Review | Final day - 11:00 | PO, TL, QA, Stakeholders | Demo increment and validate outcomes | Acceptance decisions + release notes inputs |
| Sprint Retrospective | Final day - 14:00 | PO, TL, QA, FE Team | Process improvement actions | Action items with owners and due dates |

## Tracking Notes
- Any missed ceremony must include reason and reschedule date.
- Retro actions must be converted into backlog tasks within 1 business day.
- Sprint closes only when actual completed story count and target status are recorded.
